# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in BAR to be respectful, constructive, and free from
harassment. We welcome contributors and users regardless of background,
identity, experience, or perspective.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent, discuss ideas on their merits, and give actionable
  feedback.
- Respect differing technical and lived experiences.
- Keep public discussions appropriate for a professional open-source project.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption,
or publication of private information without permission are not acceptable.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report concerns privately through the repository owner's
[GitHub profile](https://github.com/primitive-0rigins). Reports will be handled
discreetly and may result in moderation, removal of content, or restricted
participation when necessary to protect the community.

This code applies to all project spaces and public interactions representing
BAR.
